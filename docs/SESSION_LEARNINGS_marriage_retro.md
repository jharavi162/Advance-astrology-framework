**A. Durability call (#2).**
- *Error:* Let **one** strong protector (exalted Jupiter ruling/aspecting the sustenance axis) **veto a whole stack** of separative markers (UL-in-8th, debilitated Moon on UL, Ketu on UL, weak Mars).
- *And:* Ran the KP separation test only on the **cusp sub-lords** (7/2/11 → clean 1,2,7) and **never on the running daśā-lords**. Had I checked them, **Rahu → KP [12,6,10]** and **Saturn → [8,5,10,11,12]** scream separation.
- *Broken rule:* §4C-2 — *promise* is owned by the cusp sub-lord; **timing/negation is owned by the running chain.** I read promise, skipped the trigger.

**B. Hard-gated 2027 remarriage out (#5).**
- *Error:* Treated **Rahu AD → KP 6,12** as a yes/no **veto** ("can't marry in the divorce AD").
- *Truth:* Rahu's 6,12 was the **TYPE**, not a denial — **court marriage** (registrar/legal, no ceremony) + the native's **confusion/unsettledness**. The real trigger was one drill deeper: **Venus–Venus–Venus sūkṣma + Moon prāṇa** (Vivāha-Saham lord) on 29 Jan 2027, with transit **Venus in 8th** + **Saturn in 12th** (court/away/hidden), Nārāyaṇa+Chara **Libra (7th)** active.
- *Broken rules:* §4C-2 / COMMON-MISS #8 — *"drill to the sūkṣma BEFORE rejecting a date; a negating AD is not an automatic veto."* I stopped at AD.

**C. Conflated sub-events (#5 follow-on).**

## 4. Durable method lessons (carry forward)

1. **Drill to sūkṣma/prāṇa BEFORE rejecting any candidate date.** Most of my misses die here. The Venus³ trigger on 29-Jan-2027 was invisible at AD level.
2. **A "negating" KP lord usually sets the TYPE/texture, not a veto.** Rahu(6,12) on a marriage date = *court / sudden / unsettled marriage*, not *no marriage*. Read type, timing and sub-event **separately**, then synthesize.
3. **Never let one strong factor veto a stack.** Exalted Jupiter softened but did **not** prevent the divorce; the separative stack won.
4. **Always run KP on BOTH layers:** cusp sub-lord (promise) **and** the running MD/AD/PD lords (trigger/negation). Skipping the second flipped the durability call.
5. **Separate sub-events explicitly:** wedding vs stabilization vs romance; court vs ceremonial. Same year can hold different sub-events with different significators.
6. **Prefer the lord's-sign double-transit band** as the timing envelope over a prettier micro-window.
7. **No single rule is the judge.** Triangulation = let KP give *type*, daśā-sūkṣma give *date*, slow-transit give *envelope*, Saham/Jaimini give *corroboration* — and **combine**, never let one rule fix the verdict. (This was the user's core correction.)
